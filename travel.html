Travel & Humanity 🌏

From golden temples in Thailand to the winding bazaars of Istanbul, travel has shaped how I connect, learn, and dream. It’s more than a passport stamp — it’s a path to perspective, purpose, and unexpected joy.

I’m passionate about culture, community, and curiosity. My travels inspire not only my business and design work, but also how I show up in the world — with empathy, enthusiasm, and the urge to create.
