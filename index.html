My Disability Journey 🧠

Living with complex, chronic health conditions hasn’t stopped me from pursuing creative and strategic excellence — it's shaped my perspective and deepened my empathy.

From Ehlers-Danlos syndrome and POTS to neurological symptoms connected to a rare gene variant, my experience navigating the medical system has informed my resilience, my accessibility values, and the way I build teams, workflows, and brands.

I’m documenting my experience and supporting medical discovery through my Medical Imaging & Diagnostic Journey, a public project aimed at connecting rare patterns and advocating for better clinical understanding.
