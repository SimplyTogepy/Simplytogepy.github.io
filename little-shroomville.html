Welcome to Little Shroomville 🍄

A magical 5-acre homestead blooming in the heart of Illinois — Little Shroomville is where sustainability, storytelling, and tiny wonders grow.

Here, every duck and daisy has a name. We believe in low-impact farming, organic curiosity, and reconnecting people to the enchantment of the earth. Our dream is to grow into a community hub for gentle agriculture, fairytale gardening, and creative sustainability workshops.
